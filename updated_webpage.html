# Reverse a linked list in-place
def reverse_list(head):
    prev, curr = None, head
    while curr:
        next_node = curr.next
        curr.next = prev
        prev = curr
        curr = next_node
    return prev

About Phoenix Algo

Founded in 2024 by a group of passionate computer science students, Phoenix Algo was born out of the frustration with traditional coding education. We noticed that most learning platforms didn't prepare students for the realities of technical interviews and real-world development.

Our unique peer-mentorship model connects learners with instructors who have recently gone through the same challenges. This creates a more empathetic and effective learning environment where concepts are taught with practical, up-to-date industry relevance.

With over 5,000 students trained and a 92% satisfaction rate, we're proud to be changing the way coding is taught. Our alumni now work at top tech companies including Google, Amazon, and Microsoft.
